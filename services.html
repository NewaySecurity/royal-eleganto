Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

What is included in a basic website package?

Our basic website package includes up to 5 pages of professionally designed content, responsive design for all devices, basic SEO optimization, contact form integration, and all necessary technical setup. We also provide training on how to make simple content updates.

How long does it take to design a logo?

Our logo design process typically takes 1-2 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. This includes the discovery phase, concept development, refinement based on your feedback, and final file preparation. Rush services are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer payment plans for larger projects?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger projects. Typically, we require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For website and e-commerce projects, we can arrange a three-part payment schedule. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What file formats will I receive for my logo?

Our logo packages include all the file formats you'll need for both print and digital use. This includes vector formats (AI, EPS, SVG) for scalability, as well as raster formats (PNG, JPG, PDF) in various sizes and resolutions. We also provide both color and monochrome versions for different applications.

Do I need to provide content for my website?

While you can provide your own content, we also offer professional copywriting services to create compelling content for your website. Our writers will work with you to understand your business and craft engaging text that resonates with your audience. We can also source high-quality stock images or arrange professional photography if needed.

What is the difference between the hosting plans?

Our hosting plans differ in terms of storage space, bandwidth, email accounts, and support level. The Lite plan is suitable for small business websites with moderate traffic. The Standard plan works well for growing businesses with more content and traffic. The Elite plan is designed for high-traffic sites, e-commerce stores, and businesses that require maximum reliability and performance.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance for websites?

Yes, we offer various maintenance plans to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These plans include regular software updates, security monitoring, content updates, technical support, and performance optimization. We recommend a maintenance plan for all websites to ensure they remain secure and functional over time.

How do I get started with a project?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us through our contact form, email, or phone to discuss your project needs. We'll schedule an initial consultation to understand your requirements, timeline, and budget. After this, we'll provide a detailed proposal and quote. Once approved, we'll collect the initial deposit and begin the creative process.
