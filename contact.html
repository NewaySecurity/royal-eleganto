Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What is the typical response time for inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly.

Do you offer consultations before starting a project?

Yes, we offer complimentary initial consultations to discuss your project needs, timeline, and budget. This can be done via phone, WhatsApp, or through video conferencing.

What information should I prepare before contacting you?

To make our initial conversation most productive, it's helpful to have a general idea of your project scope, timeline, budget parameters, and any design preferences or examples of work you admire. This helps us understand your vision more quickly.

Do you work with clients outside of South Africa?

Absolutely! We work with clients globally and have experience managing remote projects efficiently. We use various digital collaboration tools to ensure smooth communication and project delivery regardless of location.
